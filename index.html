About Me

Hi! I’m Supawit Sittiboripat, a student in the SMT (Science-Math-Technology) program with a strong passion for both photography and web development. I’ve been working as a photographer for over 3 years, capturing moments and telling stories through my lens. Alongside photography, I’ve also been developing my skills in programming and web design. I have experience with languages such as C, Python, HTML, and CSS, and I enjoy building websites that are both functional and visually appealing. My goal is to become a computer engineer and a professional web developer. I’m always eager to learn new technologies and improve my craft whether it’s through writing clean code or finding the perfect shot. In my free time, you’ll often find me behind the camera or experimenting with new web design ideas.
